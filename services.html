Our Services in Detail

College Admissions

The journey to a music degree is as unique as your talent. Our admissions consulting service guides you through every step—from selecting the right programs to submitting polished applications and preparing for auditions. The admissions process can be complicated and competitive; having an advisor who understands the landscape can make all the difference.

  • Identifying best‑fit colleges, conservatories and universities
  • Developing an application timeline and checklist
  • Crafting compelling personal statements and résumés
  • Preparing audition repertoire, portfolios and recordings
  • Simulating mock interviews and auditions to build confidence

Project Assistance

Your creative work tells a story about who you are as a musician. We support projects that showcase your skills—whether you’re producing demo recordings, composing original pieces or assembling a portfolio for music production or songwriting programs. Our guidance ensures that each project aligns with your goals and meets the technical standards of your target schools.

  • Brainstorming and planning creative projects
  • Setting milestones and managing timelines
  • Connecting you with industry‑experienced collaborators where appropriate
  • Refining your work for maximum impact in admissions portfolios

Guitar Tutoring

Strong instrumental skills are essential for competitive music programs. Our private guitar tutoring helps you master technique and develop musicality across genres. Whether you’re a beginner learning chords or an advanced player preparing for conservatory auditions, we offer personalised lessons to strengthen your performance and expression.

  • Foundational technique: posture, finger placement and tone
  • Repertoire development across classical, jazz and contemporary styles
  • Improvisation and theory to deepen your musical understanding
  • Performance preparation and stage presence coaching
