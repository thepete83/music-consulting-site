About Us

Jin Cho Music is a boutique advisory practice founded by a professional guitarist and educator with over a decade of experience helping students gain admission to top music programs.

Our mission is to demystify the college admissions process for musicians. Drawing on extensive knowledge of conservatories, universities and liberal‑arts programs, we help you identify the schools that align with your musical aspirations. Our approach is informed by best practices in consulting—your online presence should clearly showcase expertise and build trust.

As a guitarist and educator, our founder understands the unique challenges musicians face. In addition to admissions guidance, we provide personalised guitar tutoring and support for creative projects such as recordings and portfolios. Whether you need help selecting repertoire, polishing your technique or preparing for auditions, we offer the one‑on‑one support you deserve.

  • 10+ years of college admissions consulting experience
  • Specialised expertise in music programs and auditions
  • Individualised mentorship for guitarists at all levels
  • Success guiding students to renowned conservatories and universities
